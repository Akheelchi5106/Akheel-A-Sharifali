About Me

Akheel, an M.ENGG graduate in Construction Engineering and Management from Illinois Institute of Technology, possesses over 2 years of experience in roles such as Owner Representative and Estimator, generating over $110 million in revenue. Specializing in Construction Contract Administration, Construction Cost Estimation, BIM, Claims, and project management, Akheel has demonstrated success in leading and developing teams, with proficiency in communication.

In terms of education, Akheel completed a Master of Engineering in Construction Engineering and Management, with relevant coursework covering areas such as Construction Planning, Quantity Surveying, Project Budgeting, Contract Administration, and more. Notable honors and awards include the ASCE Scholarship Award and the CMAA Chicago Chapter Scholarship Award.

In terms of work experience, Akheel served as a Construction Project Estimator at CPMH Construction, where responsibilities included supervising construction project estimation, conducting quality control measures, and coordinating with suppliers and vendors. As an Estimator-Intern at GMA Construction Group, Akheel collaborated on refining scope details, evaluating subcontractor proposals, and enhancing project outcomes through process improvements. Additionally, as a Preconstruction Intern at Blinderman Construction, Akheel assisted in bid proposal tracking, reduced rework, and provided technical assistance in project scope management.

Akheel's skills encompass various project management and BIM tools such as Microstation, Primavera P6, AutoCAD, Autodesk Revit, and others. Certified in Procore and OSHA, Akheel also demonstrates strong interpersonal skills, problem-solving abilities, and time management.

Akheel's comprehensive experience in cost estimation, project management, and BIM, along with a proven track record of delivering results in construction projects. With a focus on effective communication, team leadership, and continuous improvement, Akheel is well-equipped to contribute to any projects effectively.
